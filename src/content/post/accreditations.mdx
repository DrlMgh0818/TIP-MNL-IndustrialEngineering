<h2 class="text-center text-secondary">Accreditations and Certifications</h2>

The Technological Institute of the Philippines (T.I.P.) is renowned for its commitment to excellence in engineering and computing education, as evidenced by its prestigious accreditations and awards.

<img src="https://singlecolorimage.com/get/ffff00/400x100" alt="ABET Accreditation" class="mx-auto w-full" />

<h3 class="text-center text-secondary">ABET Accreditation</h3>

ABET, a globally respected not-for-profit organization, has recognized T.I.P. for its high educational standards, accrediting 20 of T.I.P.'s engineering and computing programs. This accreditation is a testament to T.I.P.'s quality of curriculum, faculty, facilities, and institutional support, positioning it as a leader in technological education on an international scale.

### Engineering Programs Accredited by the Engineering Accreditation Commission (EAC) of ABET

| T.I.P. Manila                              | T.I.P. Quezon City                        |
| ------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------- |
| 1. Chemical Engineering (BSChE)            | 1. Civil Engineering (BSCE)              |
| 2. Civil Engineering (BSCE)                | 2. Computer Engineering (BSCpE)          |
| 3. Computer Engineering (BSCpE)            | 3. Electrical Engineering (BSEE)         |
| 4. Electrical Engineering (BSEE)           | 4. Electronics Engineering (BSECE)       |
| 5. Electronics Engineering (BSECE)         | 5. Industrial Engineering (BSIE)         |
| 6. Industrial Engineering (BSIE)           | 6. Mechanical Engineering (BSME)         |
| 7. Mechanical Engineering (BSME)           | 7. Sanitary Engineering (BSSE)           |

| T.I.P. Manila                      | T.I.P. Quezon City              |

This milestone of accrediting 20 programs simultaneously is unparalleled in Philippine higher education and showcases T.I.P.'s dedication to fostering international employment opportunities for its graduates.

<h3 class="text-center text-secondary">ISO 9001:2008 Certification</h3>

The T.I.P.'s Quality Management System has earned ISO 9001:2008 Certification, reflecting its consistent commitment to meeting and exceeding customer expectations and regulatory requirements. This certification, awarded by Det Norske Veritas (DNV), covers all academic offerings and is part of T.I.P.'s continual effort for improvement and customer satisfaction.

T.I.P.'s Quality Management System includes notable components like the 5S program and KAIZEN, showcasing its status as a benchmark for quality management among academic institutions.

Through these accreditations and certifications, T.I.P. affirms its position as a leading institution for technological education, committed to excellence, innovation, and the success of its students on both a local and global scale.
